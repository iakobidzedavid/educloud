## Weekly Optimization (30-Minute Review)

**Every Tuesday:**

1. **Review Keyword Performance**
   - Sort by CTR (highest first) → Increase bids by 10-15%
   - Sort by CTR (lowest first) → Decrease bids by 20% or pause
   - Target: 80%+ of keywords with CTR >8%

2. **Review Search Terms**
   - Identify low-intent searches
   - Add 5-10 new negative keywords
   - Identify high-volume searches not in keyword list
   - Add 2-3 new keywords if intent-aligned

3. **Review Ad Copy**
   - Identify top-performing headline (highest CTR)
   - Identify bottom-performing headline (lowest CTR)
   - Pause bottom performer; test new headline variant
   - Target: 2 new headline tests every 2 weeks

4. **Review Geographic Performance**
   - Identify top-performing states (highest CTR, lowest CPC)
   - Increase bid adjustment by +10% for top performers
   - Consider pausing underperforming states if <5% CTR

## Rapid Response Troubleshooting

### Issue: 0 Impressions After 4 Hours

**Likely Cause:** Bid too low or Quality Score <5  
**Immediate Action:**
1. Check bid strategy → Increase max CPC by 50%
2. Check Quality Score → If <6, improve ad copy relevance
3. Wait 2 hours → Expect impressions to start flowing

### Issue: CTR <5% After 2 Days

**Likely Cause:** Ad copy not resonating with searchers  
**Immediate Action:**
1. Review bottom-performing headlines
2. Test new headline variants (focus on pain points)
3. Example: Replace "Alumni Data Made Simple" with "Track Alumni ROI Instantly"
4. Rotate ads to show new variants

### Issue: CPC >$50

**Likely Cause:** High competition or low Quality Score  
**Immediate Action:**
1. Check Quality Score → Improve to 8+ if possible
2. Use search term report to add negative keywords
3. Reduce bid on low-intent keywords
4. Focus budget on high-intent keywords (accreditation)

### Issue: 0 Conversions After 48 Hours

**Likely Cause:** Conversion tracking not set up correctly  
**Immediate Action:**
1. Test form submission (confirm GTM fires)
2. Check Google Ads conversion code (verify deployment)
3. Wait 24 hours (conversion data delay)
4. Contact Google support if issue persists

## Go/No-Go Decision Framework

### When to Scale (Increase Budget)

✅ **Go Criteria:** Met 2+ of the following
- CTR 10%+
- CPA $200-250 (within target)
- Conversion rate 2%+
- Quality Score 8+ on 80%+ keywords
- Lead quality feedback: "Excellent" or "Very Good" from sales

**Action:** Increase daily budget to $75-100 (50% increase)

### When to Optimize (Keep Budget, Improve Performance)

⚠️ **Optimize Criteria:** Met 1 of the following
- CTR 5-10% (acceptable, but room to improve)
- CPA $300-400 (slightly high, but acceptable)
- Conversion rate 0.5-1.5% (acceptable, test landing page)
- Quality Score 6-7 (acceptable, but improve ad relevance)
- Lead quality feedback: "Mixed" or "Fair"

**Action:** Keep budget at $50/day; focus on optimization
- A/B test landing page variants
- Test new ad copy
- Refine keyword list
- Review every 2 weeks

### When to Pause (Stop & Redesign)

❌ **No-Go Criteria:** Met 2+ of the following
- CTR <5%
- CPA >$400
- Conversion rate <0.5%
- Quality Score <6 on majority
- Lead quality feedback: "Poor" or "Unusable"

**Action:** Pause campaign; allocate budget to redesign
1. Analyze what's not working (ad copy? landing page? targeting?)
2. Redesign failing element (A/B test before resuming)
3. Resume with reduced budget ($25/day) for test period
4. Measure improvements; decide to scale or sunset
