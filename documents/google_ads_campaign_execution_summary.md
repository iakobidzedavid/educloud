## Risk Mitigation & Contingency Plans

### Risk: Low Conversion Rate (< 2%)
**Mitigation**:
- Pre-launch landing page testing (heat maps, user testing)
- Simple form (3 fields max) to reduce friction
- Clear value proposition above the fold

**Contingency**:
- Implement urgent landing page redesign
- Reduce form to 2 fields minimum
- Pause campaign until conversion rate improves

### Risk: High Cost Per Conversion (> $400)
**Mitigation**:
- Careful keyword selection with high intent focus
- Negative keywords to filter irrelevant clicks
- Regular bid optimization

**Contingency**:
- Pause low-converting keywords immediately
- Increase negative keyword list
- A/B test new landing page variations

### Risk: Conversion Tracking Not Working
**Mitigation**:
- Test tracking with dummy form submissions pre-launch
- Verify conversion tags in GTM and Google Ads
- Regular verification during first week

**Contingency**:
- Implement direct conversion pixel as backup
- Manual lead tracking via CRM during troubleshooting
- Contact Google Ads support if tracking issues persist

### Risk: Budget Exhaustion Without Results
**Mitigation**:
- Daily budget cap at $25 prevents major overspend
- Weekly monitoring of spend vs. performance
- Pause underperforming keywords quickly

**Contingency**:
- Reduction of ad group count to focus spend
- Pivot to higher-intent keywords only
- Request budget hold while optimizing
