3. **Conversion Tracking Setup (Foundation)**
   - Critical dependency for Target CPA optimization
   - Without tracking: Campaign runs blind; no performance optimization possible
   - Action: Complete conversion tracking BEFORE campaign activation

### Top 3 Risk Factors (Ranked by Impact)

1. **Low Search Volume** (Keywords have insufficient demand)
   - Mitigation: Expand BROAD match; add competitor keywords
   - Contingency: Reduce budget to $25/day; shift to content marketing

2. **Poor Landing Page Quality** (High bounce rate, low conversions)
   - Mitigation: A/B test landing page variants; ensure mobile optimization
   - Contingency: Pause campaign; redesign landing page; relaunch

3. **High Cost Per Click** (Competing ads drive prices up)
   - Mitigation: Improve Quality Score (8+); use negative keywords; focus on EXACT match
   - Contingency: Reduce budget; shift to organic + content marketing
