## Keyword Match Type Strategy

### Match Type Recommendations by Category

**EXACT Match** (Most Restrictive - Highest Quality Score, Lowest Volume):
- Use for high-intent, direct-intent keywords
- Primary keywords: alumni outcome tracking, alumni success tracking, accreditation support tools
- Expected CTR: 3-5%
- Expected Conversion Rate: 8-12%
- Estimated Reach: 100-300 monthly searches

**PHRASE Match** (Moderate Specificity - Balanced Volume & Quality):
- Use for secondary and tertiary keywords where word order matters
- Secondary keywords: impact measurement higher education, institutional effectiveness measurement
- Expected CTR: 2-3%
- Expected Conversion Rate: 5-8%
- Estimated Reach: 300-800 monthly searches

**BROAD Match** (Most Permissive - Highest Volume, Requires Monitoring):
- Use selectively for brand awareness and related concept discovery
- Monitor performance and add negative keywords as needed
- Examples: alumni success stories, educational impact measurement
- Expected CTR: 1-2%
- Expected Conversion Rate: 2-5%
- Estimated Reach: 500-2000+ monthly searches

### Negative Keywords (Critical for Budget Efficiency)
Add these negative keywords to prevent irrelevant spend:

- "student alumni" (targets current student content)
- "free alumni" (budget-conscious searches)
- "alumni forum" (community, not commercial intent)
- "alumni facebook" (social, not solution-seeking)
- "alumni social media" (platform, not solution)
- "alumni association" (existing, not shopping)

## Campaign Management & Optimization

### Weekly Tasks
1. **Monitor Performance**: Check impressions, clicks, CTR
2. **Quality Score Review**: Identify low-scoring keywords (< 5)
3. **Budget Pacing**: Ensure spending aligns with daily budget
4. **Conversion Tracking**: Verify pixels are firing correctly

### Bi-Weekly Optimization
1. **Keyword Performance Analysis**: 
   - Pause keywords with CTR < 1%
   - Pause keywords with Cost Per Conversion > $300
2. **Ad Copy Testing**: A/B test different headlines and descriptions
3. **Bid Adjustment Review**: Adjust bids on high-performing keywords
4. **Negative Keyword Addition**: Add search term negatives

### Monthly Strategic Review
1. **Campaign ROI Analysis**: 
   - Conversions × $100 (estimated value) vs. spend
   - Target ROI: 3:1 or higher
2. **Keyword Performance Report**: 
   - Top 10 converting keywords
   - Keywords to pause or adjust bids
3. **Budget Reallocation**: Increase budget if ROI > 3:1
4. **Reporting to Stakeholders**: Summary of leads generated
