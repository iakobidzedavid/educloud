## 📈 Daily Monitoring Checklist (5 minutes)

**Time:** 9:00 AM ET  
**Access:** Google Ads Dashboard > Campaigns > Alumni Impact Measurement

### ✅ Must Check Daily
- [ ] **Budget Status:** Verify ~$10 spent (Alert if >$12 or <$7)
- [ ] **Impressions:** 50-150 expected (Alert if <30 or >200)
- [ ] **Clicks:** 2-10 expected (Alert if <2)
- [ ] **Conversions:** Check for any form submissions
- [ ] **Ad Status:** Campaign active (not paused)

### 🔴 Red Flags
- No impressions after 24 hours → Bids may be too low
- Zero clicks after 100+ impressions → Ad relevance issue
- Spend >$15/day → Budget overspending (reduce bids)
- Quality Score <5 → Ad/keyword mismatch

## 📅 Weekly Monitoring (Mondays, 30 minutes)

| Check | Action | Target |
|-------|--------|--------|
| **Top Keywords** | Review by clicks; increase bids on high-performing | Top 3 should have CTR >2.5% |
| **Bottom Keywords** | Pause if 0 impressions >48 hours; reduce bids if CTR <1% | Bottom 5 keywords reviewed |
| **Conversions** | Count weekly conversions; calculate cost per lead | Target: 1-3 conversions/week |
| **Quality Score** | Check average QS; identify and pause low-score keywords | Maintain avg 7+ |
| **Search Terms** | Review search term report; add irrelevant searches as negative keywords | Remove 3-5 off-topic terms |

### Weekly Bid Optimization
- **Increase Bid +20%:** Keywords with CTR >3% AND conversions
- **Decrease Bid -20%:** Keywords with CTR <1.5%
- **Pause Keyword:** 0 impressions after 48 hours OR QS <4

## 🔍 Conversion Tracking

### How It Works
1. User clicks Google Ad
2. User completes contact form on edu.cloud
3. Form submission triggers conversion pixel
4. Conversion attributed to keyword that generated click
5. Data appears in Google Ads dashboard within 24 hours

### Verification
**Test Form Submission:**
1. Submit form with test data
2. Wait 24 hours
3. Check Google Ads > Conversions column
4. Should show +1 conversion

### Troubleshooting
- No conversions showing? → Check conversion pixel installed (Ctrl+F "gtag" on form page)
- Form submitting but no conversion? → Verify thank you page has conversion code
- More details? → See "Conversion Tracking Implementation Guide"

## 🎓 Glossary

**Ad Group:** Organizing unit within campaign; contains keywords and ads

**Bid:** Maximum amount willing to pay per click on keyword

**Click-Through Rate (CTR):** (Clicks / Impressions) × 100; percentage of people who see ad and click

**Cost per Click (CPC):** Average cost paid per click

**Cost per Conversion (CPA):** Total spend / Total conversions; cost to acquire each lead

**Conversion:** User completes desired action (form submission) after clicking ad

**Impression:** Ad is shown in search results

**Keyword:** Search term user enters that triggers your ad

**Quality Score:** Google's rating of keyword/ad/landing page relevance (1-10 scale)

**Responsive Search Ad:** Ad format where Google tests different headline/description combinations
