## Success Criteria & Thresholds

### Expand the Campaign ✅
**Trigger:** Cost per conversion <$50 + ≥5 conversions in prior week
**Action:** Increase daily budget to $15-25/day; add new keywords
**Expected Outcome:** 50% volume increase with maintained or improved economics

### Optimize the Campaign ⏸️
**Trigger:** Cost per conversion $50-100 + positive but marginal ROAS
**Action:** Bid optimization, negative keywords, landing page testing
**Expected Outcome:** Reduce CPA to <$50 within 2 weeks

### Pivot or Pause ❌
**Trigger:** Cost per conversion >$100 + no conversions after 300+ clicks
**Action:** Restructure keywords, test new messaging, or pause campaign
**Expected Outcome:** Investigate fundamental market fit issue
