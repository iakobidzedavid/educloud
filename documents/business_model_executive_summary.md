## Why These Numbers Are Conservative (Not Optimistic)

### ACV Assumption: $45,000 (Market-Tested)

- Research universities typically pay $60k-80k for alumni tracking/research
- Liberal arts colleges: $40k-50k
- Community colleges: $25k-35k
- **Weighted average $45k reflects realistic mix**; could be higher with premium positioning

### Retention Assumption: 90% Year 1

- Higher education institutions have high switching costs (data integration, stakeholder alignment)
- SaaS retention benchmarks: 90-95% for well-executed enterprise software
- **Conservative at 90%** given strong product-market fit signals

### CAC Assumption: $4,200

- Includes $330 allocation of fixed costs + $2,225 variable costs per customer
- Assumes internal sales team (not hiring expensive enterprise sales)
- **Does not assume** paid advertising, sponsorships, or external agencies
- **Already budgeted** at $42k total for 10 customers in Year 1

### Gross Margin Assumption: 85%

- Based on automated data pipeline + cloud infrastructure
- COGS primarily: cloud compute, API costs, data sources (~15%)
- **Validated** against similar B2B SaaS platforms (Helio, Portfolia)
- **Improves** with scale (fixed costs don't increase)

## Competitive Advantage: Why These Economics Are Defensible

### Comparison to Competing Solutions

| Solution Type | Typical CAC | LTV:CAC | Gross Margin | Switching Costs |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Consulting (alumni research) | High (6-12mo payback) | 2:1 | 30-40% | Low (project-based) |
| Traditional SaaS (Helio, Portfolia) | Medium (12-18mo payback) | 5:1 | 60-70% | Medium (integration) |
| **edu.cloud** | **Low (1.3mo payback)** | **32:1** | **85%** | **High (data moat)** |

### Why edu.cloud's Unit Economics Are Superior:

1. **AI-Powered Automation:** Web scraping + public records aggregation eliminates manual research costs, driving 85% margins vs. 30-50% for services-based competitors

2. **Data Switching Costs:** Once integrated into institutional planning (accreditation, advancement), switching to competitor is disruptive and expensive

3. **Predictable Revenue:** Multi-year contracts create annuity revenue stream, enabling higher LTV calculations

4. **Land-and-Expand:** Multiple revenue expansion points (new modules, additional segments, premium analytics)

## Pilot Execution: Operationalizing the Unit Economics

### The 6-Month Milestone (Month 6 Checkpoint)

**Must achieve:**
- ✓ 5+ customers signed with average ACV ≥ $42,000
- ✓ CAC per customer ≤ $5,000
- ✓ Customer NPS ≥ 40
- ✓ Data accuracy ≥ 85%

**If achieved:** Proceed immediately to Phase 2 (scale to 10 customers)  
**If not achieved:** Pause growth 30-60 days, diagnose gap, iterate

### The 12-Month Milestone (Year 1 Close)

**Must achieve:**
- ✓ 10 customers signed (or 8+ if slower pace)
- ✓ Average ACV $44,000-46,000
- ✓ CAC per customer <$4,500
- ✓ 80%+ of pilots renewing to commercial
- ✓ Achieve profitability (EBITDA positive)

**If achieved:** Year 2 is a scaling phase (target 30 customers)  
**If not achieved:** Extend pilot phase through Q1 Year 2; slow scaling

## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Financial Risks

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| ACV pressure (customer budget cuts) | 20% lower revenue | Medium | Segment by institution type; emphasize ROI |
| Churn acceleration (product gaps) | 50% lower retention | Low | Strong product roadmap; customer success |
| CAC inflation (competitive entry) | 50% higher costs | Low | Speed to market; referral program |

**Overall financial risk:** LOW. Even in worst-case scenario (ACV -20%, Retention 70%, Margin 75%), LTV:CAC remains 26:1 (healthy).

### Operational Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Product doesn't meet market need | Execute MVP validation with 2-3 early adopters before full pilot |
| Data accuracy issues | Build QA process; transparent methodology; audit trail |
| Onboarding takes too long | Create playbook; hire CS specialist; standardize process |

**Operational readiness:** MODERATE. Product fundamentals validated; GTM execution critical path.

### Market Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Competitive threat emerges | Helio, Portfolia entering market; differentiate on speed/cost/accuracy |
| Market moves to different solution | Monitor and adapt; focus on customer outcomes vs. feature parity |

**Market readiness:** STRONG. Apollo data shows 50+ qualified prospects; demand signals positive across all segments.
