## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This document establishes the framework for executing targeted cold outreach to 20 beachhead institutions identified through Apollo enrichment and engagement analysis. The campaign uses segment-specific messaging that addresses distinct pain points, organizational priorities, and decision-making processes across three higher education institution types.

The outreach strategy is grounded in market research findings from competitive positioning analysis, market sentiment monitoring, and problem-market fit validation conducted in prior research phases. Each segment receives tailored value propositions, relevant social proof, and institution-type-specific calls-to-action.

## GMAIL INTEGRATION & TRACKING SETUP

### Prerequisites for Email Execution
1. **Gmail Account:** edu.cloud company Gmail account with SMTP access
2. **Calendar Integration:** Calendly or similar for scheduling (linked in email CTAs)
3. **Email Tracking:** HubSpot, Mailchimp, or similar for open/click tracking
4. **Spreadsheet:** Tracking log with columns for: prospect name, institution, email, segment, sent date, open date, click date, reply date, call scheduled (Y/N), call date

### Email Sending Process
1. Authenticate Gmail account via OAuth/SMTP
2. Load verified prospect list (20 contacts with Apollo-enriched data only)
3. For each prospect:
   - Identify segment (Research U / LAC / CC)
   - Load appropriate email template
   - Populate personalization fields from Apollo data
   - Insert tracking pixel (if using email tracking tool)
   - Insert personalized calendar link
   - Send email
   - Log send in tracking spreadsheet with timestamp
4. Monitor for opens/clicks/replies over 14-day campaign window
5. Respond to replies within 2 hours with discovery call scheduling

### Metric Extraction from Gmail
- **Opens:** Email tracking tool (HubSpot, Mailchimp) provides open rate by recipient
- **Clicks:** Tracking tool logs link clicks (calendar link, website links)
- **Replies:** Manual review of Gmail inbox; log in tracking spreadsheet
- **Call Scheduling:** Calendar system logs scheduled meetings; cross-reference with tracking spreadsheet

## COMPLIANCE & BEST PRACTICES

### Email Best Practices
- **Personalization:** Every email addresses prospect by name and includes institution-specific reference
- **Authenticity:** All claims backed by research (competitive positioning, market sentiment, pain point validation)
- **Value-First:** Lead with prospect's pain point, not product pitch
- **Clear CTA:** Single, specific call-to-action (15-min discovery call)
- **Respect Opt-Out:** Include unsubscribe link (required by CAN-SPAM)

### Data Protection
- Do not store prospect email addresses in unsecured documents
- Use authenticated Gmail account for sending (no forwarding)
- Keep tracking spreadsheet in company cloud storage (not local)
- Comply with CAN-SPAM Act (from address, unsubscribe link, physical address)
