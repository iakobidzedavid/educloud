## CAMPAIGN STRATEGY SUMMARY

### Segment-Specific Positioning

**Research Universities (7 prospects)**
- **Primary Pain Point:** Proving research impact to donors and accreditors; scaling alumni tracking
- **Value Proposition:** "Automate Alumni Outcomes Tracking — Turn Data Into Fundraising Gold"
- **Key Messaging:** 40% higher employment rates, research-to-impact pathways, accreditation-ready data
- **CTA:** Free 100-200 alumni pilot with comprehensive outcomes report
- **Expected Outcome:** 20% reply rate, 1-2 pilots

**Liberal Arts Colleges (7 prospects)**
- **Primary Pain Point:** Proving graduate success to donors; limited IT resources for data management
- **Value Proposition:** "Prove Your Impact: Real Data About Where Your Graduates Succeed"
- **Key Messaging:** Authentic alumni success stories, career outcomes data, fundraising support
- **CTA:** Free pilot revealing where graduates work and earning power
- **Expected Outcome:** 18% reply rate, 0.5-1 pilot

**Community Colleges (6 prospects)**
- **Primary Pain Point:** Proving employer partnership ROI; tracking employment outcomes at scale
- **Value Proposition:** "Prove Your Training Works: Track Student Employment Outcomes"
- **Key Messaging:** 70%+ employment rates, program-by-program success, employer partnership data
- **CTA:** Free pilot showing employment outcomes by program
- **Expected Outcome:** 15% reply rate, 0.5 pilot

## LEARNING & OPTIMIZATION

### Key Questions to Answer During Campaign

1. **Does segment-specific messaging work?**
   - Compare open/click/reply rates across three segments
   - Identify which segment has highest engagement
   - Hypothesis: Research universities likely highest due to budget/priority

2. **Which pain point messaging resonates most?**
   - Test different pain points in follow-up emails
   - Track which subject lines get highest open rates
   - Insight to apply: highest-resonating pain point should lead in future campaigns

3. **What's the ideal email cadence?**
   - Monitor replies over time (Days 1-3, 4-7, 8-14 post-send)
   - Document when most replies arrive
   - Decide if 5-10-15 day follow-up is optimal or adjust timing

4. **How important is discovery call timing?**
   - Track time from initial email to call booking
   - Identify if certain "sweet spot" windows (e.g., Day 5-7) have higher conversion
   - Apply timing insights to next campaign

5. **What objections come up most frequently?**
   - Document all objections from calls and replies
   - Track which objections are budget-related, time-related, solution-related
   - Prepare counter-objections for messaging in future campaigns
