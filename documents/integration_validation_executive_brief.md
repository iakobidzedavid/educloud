## 5. Risk Assessment

### Platform Reliability: LOW RISK ✅
- Pica: 99.9% uptime (industry standard)
- Composio: 99.5% uptime (with redundancy)
- HyperFX: 99.9% uptime (enterprise-grade)

**Mitigation:** Monitoring dashboards + automated alerts

### Integration Complexity: MEDIUM RISK ⚠️
- OAuth configuration required (Salesforce, Gmail, LinkedIn)
- Data field mapping (CRM integration)
- Rate limiting considerations (email volume scaling)

**Mitigation:** Detailed setup runbooks + tech support resources

### Data Security: LOW RISK ✅
- All APIs use HTTPS/TLS encryption
- OAuth tokens stored securely in Composio vault
- No sensitive student data involved in initial testing
- FERPA compliance review recommended before full deployment

**Mitigation:** Credential management procedures + audit logging

### Market Competition: MEDIUM RISK ⚠️
- Competitors may also use these integrations
- SEO competitive positioning requires continuous optimization
- Rapid market changes require agile campaign iteration

**Mitigation:** Real-time monitoring + weekly optimization cycles
