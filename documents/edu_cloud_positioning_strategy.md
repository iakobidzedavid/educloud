## II. Value Proposition: Why Choose edu.cloud?

### **Primary Value Prop: "Answers the Question You Can't Ask the Surveys"**

**The Problem:**
- Your institution needs to know where alumni went and what impact they created
- Surveys get 5% response rates and take 6 months
- Enterprise platforms (Salesforce, Raiser's Edge) cost $50K+ and are overkill (you need outcomes, not fundraising)
- Accreditors (ACCJC, SACSCOC) increasingly require this data
- Your advancement office has no reliable way to answer "where did our grads go?"

**The edu.cloud Solution:**
- **Automated:** Discovers alumni employment, earnings, career progression without surveys
- **Fast:** Results in 4-8 weeks, not 6 months
- **Affordable:** $5K-$25K annually (not $50K-$100K)
- **Accurate:** AI-powered name matching across public records, employment databases, web sources
- **Compliant:** Built FERPA awareness into research design
- **Actionable:** Unlocks accreditation stories, fundraising intelligence, strategic planning data

## IV. Differentiation Dimensions: Why edu.cloud Wins

### **Dimension 1: Automation (The Unique Selling Proposition)**

**Claim:** "Only edu.cloud automates alumni outcomes discovery at scale"

**Evidence:**
- Web scraping + public records + AI name-matching = comprehensive alumni tracking without surveys
- Competitors rely on manual entry, surveys (5% response), or optional platform sign-ups
- edu.cloud's automation reduces time-to-insights from 6 months to 4-8 weeks

**Why it matters:** Institutions get tired of surveys; manual research is slow; edu.cloud eliminates the effort burden

---

### **Dimension 2: Affordability for Mid-Market**

**Claim:** "Only edu.cloud makes alumni outcomes measurement affordable for smaller institutions"

**Evidence:**
- Salesforce ($50K-$100K) targets large research universities
- Manual consulting ($25K-$100K) is a one-time event, not ongoing
- edu.cloud ($5K-$25K) enables ongoing measurement for regional colleges, community colleges, liberal arts schools
- Opens $1B+ TAM of institutions that can't afford enterprise platforms

**Why it matters:** 80% of higher ed institutions are SMB-sized; they have the problem but not the budget

---

### **Dimension 3: Compliance-Ready (FERPA Design)**

**Claim:** "edu.cloud is the only outcomes platform built with FERPA compliance at its core"

**Evidence:**
- Explicit FERPA compliance framework in research methodology
- No integration with student information systems (protects student data privacy)
- Public records and employment databases only (consents not required)
- Accreditor-friendly (reduces institutional risk)

**Why it matters:** Institutions are nervous about privacy regulations; edu.cloud removes that worry

---

### **Dimension 4: Comprehensiveness (No Blind Spots)**

**Claim:** "Only edu.cloud finds alumni employment across ALL sources"

**Evidence:**
- Surveys: 5% response rates (95% missing)
- LinkedIn: 40-60% adoption (40-60% missing)
- Handshake: Early career only (5-10 years missing)
- edu.cloud: 60-90% comprehensive discovery from web + public records + databases

**Why it matters:** Institutions need to know about ALL alumni (including those not on LinkedIn); partial data tells incomplete story
