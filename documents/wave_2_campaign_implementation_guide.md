## SECTION 6: QUALIFICATION CRITERIA

### WHO TO PRIORITIZE FOR CALLS

**High Priority (Schedule Immediately):**
- [ ] Email opened + clicked + replied (warm lead)
- [ ] Replied with specific questions about features
- [ ] Mentioned accreditation cycle or current pain point
- [ ] Asked about pricing (buying signal)

**Medium Priority (Schedule Within 3 Days):**
- [ ] Email opened + clicked (engaged but not replied)
- [ ] Opened twice (higher intent)
- [ ] Has decision-making title confirmed

**Low Priority (Follow-Up Sequence):**
- [ ] Email opened only (no click)
- [ ] No engagement after 5 days
- [ ] Contact is gatekeeper (not decision-maker)

### DISQUALIFICATION CRITERIA (Don't Follow Up)

**STOP Follow-Up If:**
- [ ] Email hard-bounced (invalid address)
- [ ] Contact replied "not interested" or "unsubscribe"
- [ ] Contact is out-of-office for 30+ days
- [ ] Institution is known to have in-house system that competes
- [ ] Contact has been contacted 3x with zero engagement
