## Decision-Making Structure by Segment

### Research Universities - Decision Chain
```
CFO/Provost (Strategic initiative approval)
    ↓
VP Institutional Research (Budget authority, solution evaluation)
    ↓
Associate VP or Director of Outcomes Assessment (Implementation, champions)
    ↓
IR Staff (Day-to-day usage)
```

**Entry Point:** VP Institutional Research  
**Timeline to Decision:** 4-8 weeks (institutional research cycle)  
**Budget Authority:** VP Institutional Research

### Liberal Arts Colleges - Decision Chain
```
Dean of the College (Strategic approval for new initiatives)
    ↓
Director of Institutional Research (Budget authority, direct decision-maker)
    ↓
Assessment staff (Implementation)
```

**Entry Point:** Director of Institutional Research  
**Timeline to Decision:** 2-4 weeks (smaller institution cycles)  
**Budget Authority:** Director IR (may need Dean approval for >$50K)

### Community Colleges - Decision Chain
```
VP Academic Services / VP Student Services (Strategic initiative approval)
    ↓
Director of Institutional Effectiveness / Director of IR (Budget authority)
    ↓
Outcomes assessment staff (Implementation)
```

**Entry Point:** Director of Institutional Effectiveness or VP Academic Services  
**Timeline to Decision:** 3-6 weeks (may require state system approval)  
**Budget Authority:** VP Academic Services (state budget constraints)

## Outreach Messaging by Segment

### Research Universities (Institutions 1-8)
**Primary Pain:** Managing alumni outcomes at enterprise scale (100K-500K+ alumni)  
**Key Messengers:** VP Institutional Research / AVP Outcomes Assessment  
**Message Framework:**
- "Your custom database was built for 50K alumni. How will it scale to 500K+?"
- "Enterprise-scale outcomes tracking without hiring additional staff"
- "Capital campaign outcomes documentation in real-time, not annual reports"
- **ROI Angle:** "Replace manual processes requiring 8+ staff with automated intelligence. Free up institutional research capacity for strategic work."

### Liberal Arts Colleges (Institutions 9-15)
**Primary Pain:** Limited staff (1-3 FTE) managing significant alumni tracking (20K-33K)  
**Key Messengers:** Director of Institutional Research  
**Message Framework:**
- "Your IR director shouldn't spend 50% of time on manual survey administration"
- "Automate what's consuming your outcomes staff time"
- "Multiply the impact of your institutional research team"
- **ROI Angle:** "Recover 20-30 hours/month of staff time per FTE. Redirect to strategic alumni engagement and fundraising support."

### Community Colleges (Institutions 16-20)
**Primary Pain:** Affordability + scale (tracking 8K-60K annual students with limited budget)  
**Key Messengers:** Director of Institutional Effectiveness / VP Academic Services  
**Message Framework:**
- "Demonstrate workforce outcomes without expensive consultant reports"
- "Track employment and transfer outcomes affordably at your scale"
- "Reduce manual effort in employment verification and outcome tracking"
- **ROI Angle:** "Cost-effective outcomes tracking for state reporting, grant compliance, and workforce development documentation. No expensive consultant fees."
