## THE CRITICAL INSIGHT: Accreditation Is Your Gate Opener

**Regulatory Event**: U.S. Department of Education is overhauling college accreditation system (Jan-Apr 2026)
- New emphasis: "Data-driven student performance metrics"
- Impact: Institutions must demonstrate alumni outcomes to maintain federal funding eligibility
- Urgency: Accreditation review cycles create immediate compliance deadlines (next 12-24 months)
- Market Driver: 6 accreditors control $120B in federal aid (high stakes for institutions)

**Why This Matters for edu.cloud**:
- Accreditation compliance is Pain Point #1 (CRITICAL urgency)
- Institutions facing immediate deadline pressure (next review cycle)
- Current approach insufficient: Manual research + fragmented data = compliance risk
- edu.cloud solves this: Automated outcomes + accreditation-ready reporting

**GTM Implication**: Lead with accreditation value proposition, not fundraising ROI (though both exist)

- ✅ Good for: High-quality research
- ❌ Bad for: Scalability, speed, cost
- **Gap**: Project-based, slow, not renewable

**DIY/Homegrown** (Excel, surveys, manual LinkedIn): High staff time cost
- ✅ Good for: None (all negative)
- ❌ Bad for: Everything (staff burnout, incomplete data, stale information)
- **Gap**: 40-60+ hours/week professional staff time burden
