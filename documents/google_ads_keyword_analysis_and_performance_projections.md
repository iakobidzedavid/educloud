## Executive Summary

This document provides detailed analysis of the 15 deployed keywords across 3 match types (BROAD, PHRASE, EXACT), with performance projections based on market research, Google Ads benchmarks, and higher education sector search behavior. Expected outcomes: PHRASE and EXACT keywords will drive high-intent, low-volume conversions; BROAD keywords will drive volume with lower conversion intent.

**Key Finding:** "Accreditation support tools" and "institutional effectiveness reporting" are predicted highest-performers (30%+ CTR, $10-20 CPC) due to low competition in this niche. General keywords like "alumni tracking software" will have higher CPCs ($30-50) but better volume.

## Weekly Keyword Performance Monitoring Plan

### Week 1 Monitoring Focus

**Primary Questions:**
1. Which keywords are generating impressions? (Identify volume sources)
2. Are accreditation keywords delivering high CTR as predicted?
3. Is Quality Score 7+ on core keywords?

**Actions:**
- Monitor daily impressions by keyword
- If keyword has 0 impressions after 48 hours, increase bid by 20%
- Document CTR for each keyword (compare to projections)
- Check Quality Score; if <6, review ad copy alignment

### Week 2 Optimization Focus

**Primary Questions:**
1. Which keywords are driving conversions?
2. Are conversion rates matching predictions (1.5% overall)?
3. Which keywords have highest CPA (candidates for optimization)?

**Actions:**
- Analyze conversion data by keyword
- Increase bids on high-CTR, high-conversion keywords by 20%
- Decrease bids on low-CTR keywords by 15%
- Add negative keywords based on search term report
- Update projections based on actual performance

### Week 3+ Ongoing Optimization

**Actions:**
- Consolidate best-performing keywords; allocate more budget
- Pause keywords with CPA >$350 or CTR <5%
- Test new keyword variants based on search term report
- Monitor seasonal trends (especially accreditation keywords)
- Plan Phase 2 expansion keywords

The 15-keyword strategy balances high-intent niche keywords (accreditation, institutional effectiveness) with higher-volume awareness keywords (alumni tracking, data analytics). **Monitoring Priority:** Week 1 focus on accreditation keyword performance; Week 2+ focus on conversion optimization by keyword group.
