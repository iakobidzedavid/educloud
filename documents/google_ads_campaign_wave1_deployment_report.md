## Conversion Tracking Implementation

### Primary Conversion Action
**Conversion Name:** Contact Form Submission  
**Conversion Category:** Lead  
**Conversion Value:** $0 (volume-based tracking; can be updated to estimated LTV)  
**Tracking Method:** Website tag (Google Ads conversion tracking pixel)

**Conversion Tracking Setup:**
- Form submission on edu.cloud website triggers conversion pixel
- Conversion data flows into Google Ads dashboard in real-time
- Reports available in Campaign > Conversions section
- Historical data available 24 hours after implementation

### Conversion Reporting Metrics
- **Conversions:** Total number of contact form submissions
- **Conversion Rate:** (Conversions / Clicks) × 100
- **Cost per Conversion:** Total Cost / Conversions
- **View-through Conversions:** (Optional) Users who saw ad but didn't click before converting

### Expected Performance Benchmarks
Based on higher education SaaS industry standards:
- **Click-through Rate (CTR):** 2-4% (higher for institutional research terms)
- **Conversion Rate:** 5-10% (from click to form submission)
- **Cost per Click (CPC):** $0.50-$2.00 (depending on keyword competitiveness)
- **Cost per Conversion:** $5.00-$20.00 (at $10/day budget with typical conversion rates)

## Performance Monitoring & Dashboards

### Daily Monitoring Checklist
**Frequency:** Daily (recommended morning review)

**Key Metrics to Review:**
1. ✅ **Impressions** — Target: 50-150/day (monitor for budget constraints)
2. ✅ **Clicks** — Target: 2-10/day (validate keyword relevance)
3. ✅ **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** — Target: 2-4% (quality of ad/keyword match)
4. ✅ **Average CPC** — Target: $0.50-$2.00 (monitor bid strategy effectiveness)
5. ✅ **Conversions** — Target: 1-3/week during Week 1 (early indicator of message-market fit)
6. ✅ **Cost per Conversion** — Track against budget (should improve over time with optimization)

### Weekly Monitoring Checklist
**Frequency:** Every Monday (7-day cumulative review)

1. **Keyword Performance Review:**
   - Identify top 3 performing keywords by CTR and conversion rate
   - Identify bottom 3 performing keywords (candidates for pausing/bid reduction)
   - Calculate conversion value by keyword

2. **Bid Adjustment Review:**
   - Increase bids on high-performing, converting keywords by 10-20%
   - Decrease bids on low-performance keywords by 20-30%
   - Monitor impression share (target: 80%+ for top keywords)

3. **Ad Performance Review:**
   - Review best-performing headline/description combinations
   - Monitor ad relevance scores
   - Check for disapproved ads or policy violations

4. **Budget Utilization:**
   - Confirm daily budget is being fully utilized
   - Calculate run-rate and projected monthly spend
   - Adjust bids if underspending (daily budget not exceeded)

### Monthly Reporting (End of Month)
**Deliverable:** Monthly performance report with:

1. **Campaign Summary Metrics:**
   - Total Impressions, Clicks, Conversions
   - Overall CTR, Conversion Rate, CPC, Cost per Conversion
   - Total Spend vs. Budget

2. **Comparative Analysis:**
   - Week-over-week trends (performance improving/declining)
   - Keyword performance ranking (top 5, bottom 5)
   - Geographic performance (by state, if available)

3. **Optimization Recommendations:**
   - Winning keywords for bid increases + budget allocation
   - Underperforming keywords for pausing or restructuring
   - Ad copy opportunities (based on search term performance)
   - Budget optimization (increase daily budget if ROI positive)

4. **Prospect Insights:**
   - Conversion form submission summary (count, institution types)
   - Lead quality indicators (engagement on edu.cloud website)
   - Feedback themes from contact forms (pain points mentioned)

## Success Criteria for Wave 1

### Success Metrics (30-day evaluation)
- ✅ **Campaign Active:** Live and generating impressions (Pass/Fail)
- ✅ **Conversion Tracking Operational:** Form submissions attributed to Google Ads (Pass/Fail)
- ✅ **Minimum Activity:** ≥10 conversions from ads (Pass/Fail)
- ✅ **ROAS >0:** Cost per conversion ≤ $50 (pilot engagement quality threshold)
- ✅ **CTR >1.5%:** Indicates acceptable ad/keyword relevance

### Pivot/Expand Triggers
- **Expand** (increase daily budget to $20-25): If ROAS >0 and cost per conversion <$25
- **Optimize** (keyword/bid restructuring): If ROAS >0 but cost per conversion $25-50
- **Pivot** (new keywords/messaging): If ROAS <0 or cost per conversion >$50
- **Pause** (pause campaign): If no conversions after 500 clicks or CTR <1%
