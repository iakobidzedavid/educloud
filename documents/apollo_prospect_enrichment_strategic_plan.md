## Executive Summary

This strategic plan outlines the systematic approach to identify and enrich contact information for 50+ decision-makers at target higher education institutions using the Apollo.io platform. The initiative directly resolves the critical gap identified in GTM execution where prospect list claims lack verified contact data. By leveraging Apollo's comprehensive database of institutional contacts, decision-maker titles, and verified email/phone information, we will build a high-quality prospect list segmented by institution type (research universities, liberal arts colleges, community colleges, professional schools) with minimum alumni base of 500+. The final deliverable will be a CSV export with 50+ prospects containing: institution name, contact name, title, email, phone, LinkedIn URL, institution size, alumni base, and research intensity metrics. This execution directly supports the Market Validation GTM dimension (currently 3/10) and enables immediate pilot recruitment outreach.

## Apollo Search Strategy & Methodology

### Phase 1: Search Parameter Definition (Pre-Execution)

#### Search Filter 1: Institution Type Targeting
- **Query:** "Higher Education" OR "University" OR "College" OR "Professional School"
- **Apollo Field:** Organization Industry
- **Expected Results:** Broad higher ed sector foundation

#### Search Filter 2: Alumni Base Validation
- **Metric:** Organization employee count as proxy for alumni base (minimum 500+ employees indicates 500+ alumni likely)
- **Apollo Field:** Organization Size
- **Filter Range:** 500 - 50,000 employees
- **Rationale:** Institutional employee count correlates with alumni base size and institutional maturity

#### Search Filter 3: Decision-Maker Role Targeting
- **Primary Titles:** "VP Advancement", "VP Institutional Advancement", "VP Institutional Research", "Director of Institutional Research", "Chief Data Officer", "VP Alumni Relations", "Director of Alumni Relations", "VP Institutional Effectiveness", "Director of Institutional Effectiveness", "Dean of Students", "Director of Career Services"
- **Apollo Field:** Title
- **Seniority Filter:** Manager level and above
- **Rationale:** These roles directly own alumni outcome tracking, impact measurement, and fundraising initiatives

#### Search Filter 4: Geographic Focus
- **Primary:** United States
- **Secondary:** Canada, United Kingdom
- **Apollo Field:** Organization Country
- **Rationale:** US market aligns with beachhead strategy; international expansion secondary

#### Search Filter 5: Revenue/Funding Signals
- **Non-profit Status:** Verify nonprofit 501(c)(3) status
- **Apollo Field:** Organization Type / Funding Status
- **Rationale:** Ensures legitimate educational institutions with proper accreditation

### Phase 2: Systematic Search Execution

#### Search Strategy A: Segment-Specific Institution Searches
For each target institution segment, execute focused Apollo searches:

**Research Universities Search:**
- Query: "Research University" OR "R1 University" OR "Research Institution" OR "Major Research University"
- Combined with: Title filter (VP Institutional Research, Director of Institutional Effectiveness)
- Size filter: 5,000+ employees
- Expected yield: 15-20 verified prospects

**Liberal Arts Search:**
- Query: "Liberal Arts College" OR "Selective College"
- Combined with: Title filter (VP Advancement, Director Alumni Relations)
- Size filter: 1,500-3,000 employees
- Expected yield: 12-15 verified prospects

**Community College Search:**
- Query: "Community College" OR "Technical College" OR "Public College"
- Combined with: Title filter (Director Institutional Research)
- Size filter: 500-5,000 employees
- Expected yield: 10-12 verified prospects

**Professional School Search:**
- Query: "Law School" OR "Medical School" OR "Business School" OR "Graduate Program"
- Combined with: Title filter (Director Career Services, Dean of Students)
- Size filter: 300-2,000 employees
- Expected yield: 10-13 verified prospects

#### Search Strategy B: Function-Based Searches
Execute role-specific searches across all institution types:

**Advancement Function:**
- Query: "Institutional Advancement" OR "VP Advancement" OR "Development Officer"
- Expected yield: 18-22 prospects
- Value: Directly addresses fundraising pain points

**Institutional Research Function:**
- Query: "Institutional Research" OR "Data Analytics" OR "Effectiveness"
- Expected yield: 20-25 prospects
- Value: Directly addresses impact measurement pain point

**Alumni Relations Function:**
- Query: "Alumni Relations" OR "Alumni Engagement" OR "Alumni Services"
- Expected yield: 15-18 prospects
- Value: Directly addresses alumni outcome tracking pain point

### Phase 3: Data Enrichment & Verification

#### Enrichment Data Points (Per Contact)
1. **Contact Name:** Full name with verification
2. **Job Title:** Exact current title (no estimation)
3. **Institution Name:** Legal/official institution name
4. **Email Address:** Primary business email (Apollo verified)
5. **Phone Number:** Direct or main institutional phone (Apollo verified)
6. **LinkedIn Profile:** URL to professional profile for credibility verification
7. **Institution Size:** Employee count (proxy for alumni base size)
8. **Alumni Base Estimate:** Calculated from institution data
9. **Research Intensity:** Classification (High/Medium/Low based on research funding and R-rank)

#### Verification Process
- **Email Validation:** Apollo platform provides email verification status (verified/unverified)
- **Phone Validation:** Cross-reference with institution directory and LinkedIn
- **LinkedIn Confirmation:** Validate job title and timeline against LinkedIn profile
- **Title Verification:** Confirm title matches functional area (advancement, IR, alumni)
- **Accuracy Threshold:** Only include contacts with email verification status = VERIFIED or VERIFIED_CURRENT

#### Duplicate Detection & Deduplication
- Remove exact duplicate records (same contact at same institution)
- Consolidate multiple contacts at same institution (keep highest-level decision-maker)
- Flag contacts with multiple roles (e.g., dual advancement/research titles)

## Risk Mitigation & Contingency Planning

### Risk 1: Insufficient Contact Quantity from Searches
**Mitigation:** Expand searches to include alternative title variations and director-level roles; broaden institution size parameters; consider secondary US markets

### Risk 2: Low Email Verification Rates
**Mitigation:** Cross-reference with institution websites and LinkedIn; use alternative contact methods (phone verified through directory); include multiple contacts per high-priority institution

### Risk 3: Data Accuracy Issues
**Mitigation:** Manual spot-check sample of 10% of records; validate against institution websites and LinkedIn; flag records with partial data for secondary verification

### Risk 4: Outdated Contact Information
**Mitigation:** Prioritize contacts with recent LinkedIn activity; cross-reference update dates; note any contacts with job change indicators

### Risk 5: Limited Decision-Maker Access
**Mitigation:** Include secondary contacts (associate directors, managers) at high-priority institutions; document seniority levels for segmentation; prioritize C-suite and VP-level contacts
