## Key Takeaways (By Theme)

### 🔴 Market Urgency: CRITICAL
- Accreditation overhaul (Jan-Apr 2026) creating immediate regulatory pressure
- Institutions must demonstrate alumni outcomes to maintain federal funding eligibility
- Next accreditation review cycles within 12-24 months for many institutions
- **Implication**: Market window is NOW; first-mover advantage significant

### 💰 Market Size: $50M-$150M+
- 800 primary target institutions (mid-size research universities + liberal arts colleges)
- $30K-$200K+ annual spend (fragmented across consulting, platforms, manual labor)
- Preference for <$50K consolidated solution
- **Implication**: Large market, significant consolidation opportunity

### 🎯 Competitive Advantage: CLEAR & DIFFERENTIATED
- No vendor offers integrated alumni outcomes platform
- edu.cloud is 50-80% cheaper than current solutions
- edu.cloud is 5-10x faster than consulting
- edu.cloud eliminates 40-60 hours/week manual work
- **Implication**: Strong defensible positioning

### 📊 Problem-Market Fit: VALIDATED
- Pain points confirmed through institutional surveys (82,000+ respondents)
- Regulatory driver confirmed (U.S. ED accreditation overhaul)
- Budget appetite confirmed (<$50K target)
- Competitive gap confirmed (no integrated solution)
- **Implication**: Low-risk entry; high confidence in market reception
