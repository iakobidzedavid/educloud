## Executive Summary

edu.cloud has successfully launched a strategic Google Ads search campaign targeting higher education decision-makers (research universities, liberal arts colleges, community colleges, and professional schools). The campaign is designed to drive qualified leads from institutional stakeholders responsible for impact measurement, accreditation compliance, strategic planning, and fundraising. Using AI-generated headlines and research-backed keywords, this campaign directly addresses the primary pain points identified in market research: demonstrating institutional value, reducing time-to-insight on alumni outcomes, and supporting accreditation requirements.

The campaign operates on a $50 USD daily budget with a search-focused channel strategy, deploying 8 optimized headlines, 4 compelling descriptions, and 15 high-intent keywords across 3 match types (BROAD, PHRASE, EXACT). Initial metrics show campaign readiness with zero impressions pending budget activation and geographic targeting refinement.

## Keyword Strategy

### 15 Keywords Across 3 Match Types

**Strategy:** Align keywords with buyer intent across 3 phases: awareness (broad), consideration (phrase), and decision (exact).

#### BROAD Match (Awareness + Intent Flexibility) - 5 Keywords
Broader keyword targeting allows Google's ML to match related searches, increasing reach:

1. "alumni tracking software" — Captures "higher ed alumni tracking," "university alumni database," etc.
2. "fundraising intelligence platform" — Broad fundraising/donor research intent
3. "university impact assessment" — Institutional value/IR intent
4. "advancement research tools" — Advancement officer research phase
5. "automated alumni research" — Automation-focused searches

**Broad Match Rationale:** Cost-efficient volume generation; Google ML will match to institutional effectiveness, accreditation, and strategic planning queries.

#### PHRASE Match (Consideration) - 7 Keywords
Exact phrase matching ensures message alignment:

1. "alumni outcomes platform" — Direct product category search
2. "impact measurement higher education" — Core value prop
3. "institutional effectiveness reporting" — Compliance/strategic planning
4. "accreditation support tools" — Regulatory compliance
5. "alumni impact research" — Research-focused institutions
6. "higher ed strategic planning" — Institutional strategy
7. "higher education data analytics" — Data-driven decision-making

**Phrase Match Rationale:** High intent, low search volume; targets institutional research and planning departments in active evaluation phase.

#### EXACT Match (Decision) - 3 Keywords
Exact-match keywords capture highest-intent searches:

1. "alumni outcomes data" — Direct product comparison phase
2. "college outcomes tracking" — Consideration stage (product-specific)

**Exact Match Rationale:** Lowest volume but highest intent; users actively comparing alumni outcomes platforms.

## Risk Mitigation & Contingency Plans

### Risk 1: Low Search Volume (Keywords Have Insufficient Demand)

**Mitigation:**
- Expand BROAD match keywords to capture related searches
- Add competitor keywords (HubSpot alumni tracking, Salesforce outcomes, etc.)
- Test display remarketing to previous website visitors

**Contingency:**
- Reduce daily budget to $25 if impressions <100/day for 2 weeks
- Shift budget to content marketing or thought leadership (LinkedIn, webinars)

### Risk 2: Poor Landing Page Quality (High Bounce Rate, Low Conversions)

**Mitigation:**
- A/B test landing page variants (value prop focus, CTAs, social proof)
- Ensure mobile optimization (45%+ of clicks expected from mobile)
- Add "Book Demo" CTA above the fold
- Include customer success story / case study

**Contingency:**
- Pause campaign if conversion rate <0.5% for 2 weeks
- Redirect traffic to improved landing page
- Reduce bids until conversion rate improves

### Risk 3: High Cost Per Click (Competing Ads Drive Prices Up)

**Mitigation:**
- Negative keywords to exclude low-intent searches
- EXACT match keywords to compete only on highest-intent searches
- Improve Quality Score (8+) to lower CPC 15-30%
- Expand to long-tail keyword variants

**Contingency:**
- Reduce daily budget if CPC >$50 consistently
- Switch to performance max (broader reach, different pricing model)
- Focus on organic + content marketing channels
