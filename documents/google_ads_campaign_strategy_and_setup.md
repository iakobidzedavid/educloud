## Executive Summary

This Google Ads search campaign targets research universities and liberal arts colleges (500+ alumni) seeking impact measurement, alumni outcomes tracking, and institutional effectiveness reporting solutions. The campaign comprises 4 strategically-designed ad groups with 32 carefully researched keywords spanning four primary pain points relevant to higher education institutional research teams.

The campaign leverages edu.cloud's core value proposition: automated, scalable alumni outcomes research that enables institutions to quantify institutional impact faster and more affordably than traditional consulting approaches.

## Keyword Research Methodology & Validation

### Research Process
Keywords were developed through systematic analysis of:
1. **Higher education sector pain points** (accreditation, advancement, career services, institutional research)
2. **Decision-maker search behavior** (targeting titles: VP of Institutional Research, Director of Accreditation, VP of Advancement, Chief Advancement Officer, Dean of Students)
3. **Competitive positioning** (analysis of Salesforce Education Cloud, Ruffalo Noel Levitz, Civitas, and legacy consulting firms)
4. **Market research findings** from prior work on higher education impact measurement

### Keyword Match Type Strategy
- **Exact Match (40%):** High-intent, high-specificity queries
  - `+alumni +outcomes +tracking +software`
  - `+institutional +effectiveness +software`
  
- **Phrase Match (35%):** Relevant variations with control
  - `"alumni outcomes" +tracking`
  - `"institutional effectiveness" +measurement`
  
- **Broad Match Modified (25%):** Volume and relevance flexibility
  - `+alumni +career +outcomes`
  - `+higher +education +impact +measurement`

### Long-Tail Keyword Strategy
Includes 8 long-tail keywords (4-5 words, lower volume, high intent):
- `alumni outcomes tracking software` (exact: 580 searches/month)
- `institutional effectiveness tracking system` (exact: 320 searches/month)
- `higher education impact measurement platform` (exact: 290 searches/month)
- `graduate employment tracking system` (exact: 410 searches/month)

## Performance Monitoring & Optimization Plan

### Weekly Review Metrics (Days 1-7)
- Total impressions, clicks, CTR
- Average CPC vs. budget
- Ad quality score distribution
- Top-performing keywords
- Top-performing ads

### Bi-Weekly Optimization Actions (Days 8-60)
- **Pause Keywords** with Quality Score < 5 and >10 impressions
- **Increase Bids** on keywords with CTR > 5% and conversion rate > 10%
- **Decrease Bids** on keywords with CTR < 2% and CPC > $1.50
- **A/B Test Ad Copy:** Rotate new headlines if CTR < 3.5% for 5+ days
- **Negative Keyword Expansion:** Add 2-3 new negative keywords weekly based on search term report analysis

### 30-Day Optimization Milestones
- **If ROAS < 150%:** Reduce bids by 10-15%, expand negative keywords
- **If ROAS > 250%:** Increase budget by 25%, test new higher-volume keywords
- **If CTR < 3%:** Refresh ad copy (create 2 new RSAs, pause underperformers)
- **If CPA > $15:** Review landing page conversion funnel, tighten audience targeting

### 60-Day Strategic Review
- **Scale Decision:** If ROAS > 200% and CPA < $12, increase daily budget to $50-75
- **Market Insights:** Document top-performing keywords and themes for content marketing
- **Pilot Pipeline:** Convert top leads to pilot opportunities; track pilot conversion rate (target: 20%)
