## Segmentation Rules

### Liberal Arts Colleges
- **Template A (Stewardship):** Default for most liberal arts colleges; emphasizes relationship-building and alumni engagement
- **Template B (Regional Impact):** Use if institution has strong regional focus or geographic clustering of alumni
- **Template C (Peer-to-Peer):** Use for colleges with highly engaged alumni networks or strong class affinity

### Community Colleges
- **Template A (Workforce Outcomes):** Default; emphasizes employment and earnings verification
- **Template B (Transfer Success):** Use if institution has significant transfer pathway or articulation agreements
- **Template C (Employer Partnerships):** Use if institution actively partners with employers on workforce programs

**Personalization Checklist:**
- ✓ Use recipient's first name
- ✓ Reference specific institution and relevant regional/workforce context
- ✓ Connect to their specific role and institutional priorities
- ✓ Mention 1-2 comparable institutions facing similar challenges
- ✓ Lead with pain point specific to liberal arts or community college context
- ✓ Include clear 15-minute CTA with specific timeframe
- ✓ Always include phone number for easier response
