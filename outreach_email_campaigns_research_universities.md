## Segmentation Rules

- **Template A (Alumni Outcomes):** Use for institutions emphasizing alumni engagement, student success metrics, or general impact measurement
- **Template B (Accreditation):** Use for institutions with recent/upcoming accreditation cycles, mention specific regional accreditor
- **Template C (Fundraising):** Use for institutions with strong development/advancement operations and larger endowments

**Personalization Checklist:**
- ✓ Use recipient's first name (never "Dear Sir/Madam")
- ✓ Reference specific institution by name
- ✓ Mention 1-2 comparable institutions (from same institution type) that have similar challenges
- ✓ Connect pain point to their specific role (VP of Advancement = fundraising; Alumni Director = engagement)
- ✓ Include specific CTA with time frame ("Can we find 15 minutes next week?")
- ✓ Always include phone number for easier reply
